______ our story

Building a Healthier Tomorrow

Founded in 2005 by Dr. Suresh Mehta, Dehradun Hospital began as a small 50-bed facility with a simple mission: to provide quality healthcare that is accessible, affordable, and compassionate.

Over two decades, we have grown into a 200+ bed multi‑specialty hospital, serving over 10,000 patients annually. Our journey has been shaped by the trust of our patients and the dedication of our team.

______ our journey

Milestones That Define Us

From a small clinic to a multi‑specialty hospital – our story in moments.

2005

Foundation

Dehradun Hospital opens its doors with 50 beds and a team of 10 doctors.

2010

Expansion

Added 100 more beds and launched the cardiology and neurology departments.

2015

Recognition

Awarded "Best Regional Hospital" and NABH accreditation for quality standards.

2020

COVID-19 Response

Set up a dedicated 100-bed COVID care unit and served 2,000+ patients.

2024

Today

200+ beds, 150+ doctors, 10+ specialities – serving 10,000+ patients yearly.
